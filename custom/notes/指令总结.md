#### 运动

##### 操纵

```cmd
lerobot-teleoperate ^
    --robot.type=so100_follower ^
    --robot.port=COM3 ^
    --robot.id=follower_arm ^
    --teleop.type=so100_leader ^
    --teleop.port=COM4 ^
    --teleop.id=leader_arm ^
    --display_data=true ^
    --robot.cameras="{ front: {type: opencv, index_or_path: 1, width: 640, height: 480, fps: 30}}" 
```

##### 录制

```cmd
lerobot-record ^
    --robot.type=so100_follower ^
    --robot.port=COM3 ^
    --robot.id=follower_arm ^
    --teleop.type=so100_leader ^
    --teleop.port=COM4 ^
    --teleop.id=leader_arm ^
    --display_data=true ^
    --robot.cameras="{ front: {type: opencv, index_or_path: 1, width: 640, height: 480, fps: 30}}" ^
    --dataset.repo_id=Vacuame/record-test1 ^
    --dataset.num_episodes=2 ^
    --dataset.single_task="Grab the black cube" ^
    --dataset.push_to_hub=False
```

#### 数据库

##### 查看单集

```cmd
lerobot-dataset-viz --repo-id=Vacuame/record-test1 --episode-index=0
```

#### 训练

##### 训练

```cmd
lerobot-train ^
  --dataset.repo_id=Vacuame/triple_data ^
  --output_dir=outputs/train/my_policy2 ^
  --policy.repo_id=Vacuame/my_policy2 ^
  --job_name=customACT_so100test ^
  --policy.type=customACT ^
  --policy.device=cpu ^
  --policy.push_to_hub=False ^
  --wandb.enable=false ^
  --steps=100 ^
  --eval_freq=20 ^
  --log_freq=1
```

##### 检验

```cmd
lerobot-record ^
    --robot.type=so100_follower ^
    --robot.port=COM3 ^
    --robot.id=follower_arm ^
    --display_data=true ^
    --robot.cameras="{ front: {type: opencv, index_or_path: 1, width: 640, height: 480, fps: 30}}" ^

    --dataset.num_episodes=2 ^
    --dataset.single_task="Grab the black cube" ^
    --dataset.push_to_hub=False ^
    --policy.path=Vacuame/my_policy1
```

#### 装配

##### 寻找port

```cmd
lerobot-find-port
```

##### 设置电机

```cmd
# leader
lerobot-setup-motors ^
    --teleop.type=so100_leader ^
    --teleop.port=COM3
```

```cmd
# follower
lerobot-setup-motors ^
    --robot.type=so100_follower ^
    --robot.port=COM3
```

##### 校准

```cmd
# leader
lerobot-calibrate ^
--teleop.type=so100_leader ^
--teleop.port=COM4 ^
--teleop.id=leader_arm
```

```cmd
# follower
lerobot-calibrate ^
    --robot.type=so100_follower ^
    --robot.port=COM3 ^
    --robot.id=follower_arm
```
